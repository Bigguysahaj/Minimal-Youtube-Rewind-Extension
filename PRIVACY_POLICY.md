# Privacy Policy

**True Youtube Rewind Extension** does not collect, store, transmit, or share any user data of any kind.

- No personal information is collected
- No browsing history or activity is tracked
- No data is sent to any external server
- All functionality runs entirely locally within your browser

This extension only interacts with YouTube video playback controls on `www.youtube.com`. It does not access any other websites or browser data.

*Last updated: April 2026*
